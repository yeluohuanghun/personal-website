PROFILE: Shuang Wang is a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Southeast University, Nanjing, China (thesis submitted). She received her B.Sc. in the College of Sciences from the Nanjing Agricultural University in 2015. During her Ph.D. study, she focuses on designing efficient algorithms in service computing to improve the system performance. Her research has been published in top conferences and journals in related domains (e.g., ICSOC, IEEE Transactions on Computers). She has also been actively participating in key projects on service systems in China. Currently, she is a visiting scholar at Department of Computing, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia from 11, 2019 to 10, 2020.
