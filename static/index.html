Shuang Wang is a postdoc in Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia. she got her doctor's degree in 2020 from Southeast University, Nanjing, China. She received her B.Sc. in the College of Sciences from the Nanjing Agricultural University in 2015. She focuses on designing efficient algorithms in service computing to improve the system performance, cloud computing, truth discovery and reinforcement learning. Her research has been published in top conferences and journals in related domains (e.g., IEEE Transactions on Computers, ICSOC).

Main external profiles:
